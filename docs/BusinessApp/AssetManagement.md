# Asset Tracking

The Asset Management system helps you track company equipment, vehicles, and valuable assets throughout their lifecycle.

## Asset Categories

### Dive Equipment Assets
- **SCUBA Gear** - Regulators, BCDs, computers, masks, fins
- **Safety Equipment** - First aid kits, oxygen systems, rescue gear
- **Training Equipment** - Demonstration gear, training materials
- **Photography Equipment** - Underwater cameras, lighting systems

### Vehicle Assets
- **Dive Boats** - Main dive vessels and tenders
- **Transport Vehicles** - Trucks, vans for customer transport
- **Utility Vehicles** - Equipment transport and maintenance vehicles
- **Personal Vehicles** - Company cars for staff use

### Facility Assets
- **Buildings** - Dive shops, offices, storage facilities
- **Equipment** - Compressors, generators, water systems
- **Furniture** - Office furniture, customer seating
- **Technology** - Computers, servers, communication systems

### Miscellaneous Assets
- **Tools** - Maintenance tools and equipment
- **Inventory** - Retail items and consumables
- **Intellectual Property** - Software licenses, trademarks
- **Financial Assets** - Investments, accounts receivable

## Asset Registration

### New Asset Setup
When adding new assets to the system:

1. **Basic Information**
   - **Asset Name** - Clear, descriptive title
   - **Asset ID** - Unique identifier
   - **Category** - Primary classification
   - **Description** - Detailed asset description
   - **Serial Number** - Manufacturer serial number

2. **Acquisition Details**
   - **Purchase Date** - When asset was acquired
   - **Purchase Price** - Original acquisition cost
   - **Vendor** - Supplier information
   - **Invoice Number** - Purchase documentation
   - **Warranty Information** - Coverage details

3. **Location Assignment**
   - **Primary Location** - Where asset is normally kept
   - **Current Location** - Actual current location
   - **Location History** - Track movement over time
   - **Responsible Person** - Person accountable for asset

4. **Configuration**
   - **Asset Type** - Equipment, vehicle, facility, etc.
   - **Depreciation Method** - How asset value decreases
   - **Service Requirements** - Maintenance needs
   - **Usage Tracking** - How to monitor usage

## Asset Tracking

### Location Management
Track asset locations in real-time:
- **GPS Tracking** - For vehicles and mobile equipment
- **RFID Tags** - For smaller equipment items
- **Barcode Scanning** - For inventory management
- **Manual Check-out/Check-in** - For controlled access

### Usage Monitoring
Monitor how assets are used:
- **Usage Hours** - Track operating time
- **Dive Count** - For dive equipment
- **Distance Traveled** - For vehicles
- **Customer Assignments** - Who uses which assets

### Condition Assessment
Monitor asset condition:
- **Visual Inspections** - Regular physical checks
- **Performance Metrics** - Operational efficiency
- **Maintenance History** - Service and repair records
- **Damage Reports** - Incident and damage documentation

## Maintenance Integration

### Service Scheduling
Connect asset tracking to maintenance:
- **Automatic Reminders** - Based on usage or time
- **Service History** - Complete maintenance records
- **Cost Tracking** - Maintenance expenses per asset
- **Downtime Tracking** - When assets are unavailable

### Lifecycle Management
Manage asset throughout its life:
- **Acquisition** - Purchase and setup
- **Deployment** - Put into service
- **Maintenance** - Ongoing service and repair
- **Upgrade** - Improvements and modifications
- **Disposal** - Retirement and replacement

### Replacement Planning
Plan for asset replacement:
- **Depreciation Tracking** - Monitor value decline
- **Performance Decline** - Identify aging assets
- **Technology Obsolescence** - Plan for upgrades
- **Budget Planning** - Prepare for replacement costs

## Financial Management

### Depreciation
Track asset value over time:
- **Straight Line** - Equal depreciation each year
- **Declining Balance** - Higher depreciation early on
- **Units of Production** - Based on usage
- **Custom Methods** - Business-specific depreciation

### Cost Analysis
Monitor asset-related costs:
- **Total Cost of Ownership** - All costs over asset life
- **Cost Per Use** - Cost per dive, hour, or mile
- **Maintenance Costs** - Service and repair expenses
- **Operating Costs** - Fuel, supplies, consumables

### Budget Impact
Assess asset impact on budgets:
- **Capital Expenditures** - New asset purchases
- **Operating Expenses** - Ongoing asset costs
- **Replacement Budgets** - Future replacement needs
- **Disposal Proceeds** - Value from retired assets

## Reporting and Analytics

### Asset Utilization
Monitor how assets are used:
- **Utilization Rates** - Percentage of time assets are in use
- **Idle Time** - When assets are not being used
- **Peak Usage Times** - When assets are most needed
- **Bottleneck Analysis** - Identify constrained resources

### Performance Metrics
Track asset performance:
- **Reliability** - Percentage of time assets are available
- **Efficiency** - Output per unit of input
- **Quality** - Defect rates and customer satisfaction
- **Safety** - Incident rates and safety compliance

### Financial Reports
Generate financial insights:
- **Asset Value Reports** - Current and historical values
- **Depreciation Schedules** - Value decline over time
- **Cost-Benefit Analysis** - ROI on asset investments
- **Budget Variance** - Actual vs. planned costs

## Security and Access Control

### Access Management
Control who can access assets:
- **User Permissions** - Define access levels
- **Check-out Procedures** - Formal asset assignment
- **Return Requirements** - Asset return conditions
- **Audit Trails** - Track all asset movements

### Security Measures
Protect valuable assets:
- **Physical Security** - Locks, alarms, surveillance
- **Tracking Systems** - GPS, RFID, barcode systems
- **Insurance Coverage** - Protect against loss or damage
- **Theft Prevention** - Anti-theft measures and procedures

### Compliance Requirements
Meet regulatory standards:
- **Safety Standards** - Equipment safety compliance
- **Environmental Regulations** - Environmental impact compliance
- **Industry Standards** - Professional association requirements
- **Legal Requirements** - Local, state, federal regulations

## Best Practices

### Asset Organization
1. **Consistent Naming** - Use standardized naming conventions
2. **Clear Categories** - Logical asset classification
3. **Complete Documentation** - Maintain detailed records
4. **Regular Audits** - Verify asset existence and condition

### Data Management
1. **Accurate Data** - Ensure information is correct and current
2. **Regular Updates** - Keep asset information up to date
3. **Backup Systems** - Protect asset data from loss
4. **Data Validation** - Verify data accuracy regularly

### Process Optimization
1. **Streamlined Workflows** - Efficient asset management processes
2. **Automation** - Use technology to reduce manual work
3. **Integration** - Connect with other business systems
4. **Continuous Improvement** - Regularly review and improve processes

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues
- **Missing Assets** - Check location history and recent assignments
- **Data Inconsistencies** - Verify data entry and update procedures
- **Tracking Failures** - Check tracking equipment and batteries
- **Access Problems** - Review user permissions and procedures

### System Issues
- **Sync Problems** - Check network connections and data synchronization
- **Performance Issues** - Optimize database and system performance
- **Reporting Errors** - Verify data sources and report parameters
- **Integration Failures** - Check system connections and APIs
