# Shopify POS

## Purpose

To provide step-by-step instructions for using the Shopify POS system for all retail transactions, including sales, returns, exchanges, and cash management.

## Scope

This SOP applies to all staff members who operate the Shopify POS system at the retail shop or harbor office.

## Procedure

### Opening the Register

1. **Enter your PIN**: Use your 4-6 digit PIN.
2. **Click on the "More" tab**.
3. **Select "Register"**.
4. **Click on "Start Tracking Session"**.
5. **Enter the amount of cash** in the register.
6. **Click on "Start Tracking"**.
7. **Count the cash** in the drawer to make sure it matches what the float states.
8. **Save and verify** Open Session.

!!! note
    If you do not have a PIN, notify management so they can set you up with one.

!!! note
    If the amount differs from the previous closing balance, correct it and add a note for the variance. Notify management.

### Adding Customers

1. **Click on "Add Customer"**.
2. **Search for the customer** in the system before adding a new one.
    - If not found, click on "Add New Customer".
    - Enter First and Last Name. Email/phone is optional for digital receipts.
    - Click "Save".

![SearchCustomer](../Images/POS/AddCustomer.jpg)

### Making a Sale

1. **Stickers/Magnets**: Click on the "Stickers/Magnets" tab, select the type, then select the specific item. This adds it to the cart.
2. **Search for items**: Click on the "Magnifying Glass" icon to search, or click the "Barcode" icon to scan with the camera.

!!! warning
    Make sure you select the correct color/size when selecting items, otherwise the inventory will be incorrect.

![Stickers](../Images/POS/DifferentVariants.jpg)
![Cart](../Images/POS/ItemInCart.jpg)

3. **Adjust quantities**: If the customer is buying multiple of the same size and color, click on the item to adjust quantities.
4. **Click "Checkout"** when ready.
5. **Card payments**: Have the customer insert/tap the card on the card reader.
6. **Cash payments**: Click on "Cash" and enter the amount received.
7. **Receipt**: Ask the customer if they would like a printed or digital receipt. If digital, ask email or text and have them enter the information.

### Adding Cash to Register

!!! note
    This is mainly used for Checkfront Reservations paid in cash.

1. **Click on the "More" tab**.
2. **Select "Register"**.
3. **Click on "View Open Session"**.
4. **Click on "Add Cash"**.
5. **Enter the amount received**: If the customer pays with $100 for a $60 ticket, enter $60.
6. **Make a note** with the customer's name and what the money was for (e.g., "Joe Blow Diving").
7. **Save** and add cash to the register.

![ViewOpenSession](../Images/POS/OpenTrackingSession.jpg)
![AddingCash](../Images/POS/AddingCash.jpg)

### Removing Cash from Register

!!! note
    This is mainly used for Refunds or Online Order Shipping Costs.

1. **Click on the "More" tab**.
2. **Select "Register"**.
3. **Click on "View Open Session"**.
4. **Click on "Remove Cash"**.
5. **Enter the amount** to be removed.
6. **Make a note** for the reason (e.g., "Joe Blow Online Order Shipping").
7. **Save** and remove the cash from the register.

![RemovingCash](../Images/POS/RemovingCash.jpg)

### Returns

!!! note
    Returns are allowed for items that still have the tags attached **(NOT Worn or Washed)**.

1. **Click on the "Orders" tab**.
2. **Find the order**: Scan the receipt barcode, or search by name or Order Number.
3. **Click on their order**.
4. **Click on "Return"**.
5. **Select the items** to return.
6. **Click "Next"**.
7. **Select the reason** for return and add a note if applicable.
8. **Click "Next"**.
9. **Refund**: The system automatically selects the original payment method. Card payments are refunded to card; cash is only refunded if they paid with cash.
10. **Click "Refund"** to complete.

### Exchanging Items

!!! note
    Same rule applies as returns â€” items must have tags attached **(NOT Worn or Washed)**.

1. **Follow the same steps as Returns** until you get to their order.
2. **Click on "Exchange"**.
3. **Select the reason** for exchange with a note.
4. **Select the new item** they would like to exchange for.
5. **Add more items**: If the customer wants to add more items, click "Add More Items".
6. **Click "Next"** when complete.
7. **Confirm Exchange Summary** by clicking "Exchange".
8. **Receipt**: Print/text/email the new receipt. If more money is needed, follow steps to take payment.

### Employees Charging on Account

1. **Select the employee** as the Customer.
2. **Add items** to the cart.
3. **Click "Checkout"**.
4. **Click on "Mark As Unpaid"**.
5. **Confirm** by selecting "Mark As Unpaid" again.

!!! warning
    When charging to account, there is **NO** employee discount. Items must be charged at full price.

![MarkUnpaid](../Images/POS/MarkAsUnpaid.jpg)

### Employee Discount

!!! note
    Employee must pay with cash or card to receive the discount. Not applicable when charging to account.

1. **Select the employee** as a customer.
2. **Add items** to the cart.
3. **Click on one of the items**.
4. **Click on "Discount"**.
5. **Enter 40%** in the Percentage box.
6. **Reason for discount**: Type "Employee".
7. **Click "Save"** twice.
8. **Continue** with the checkout process.

![Discount](../Images/POS/ChangeQty.jpg)

### Closing the Register

1. **Click on the "More" tab**.
2. **Select "View Open Session"**.
3. **Click on "End Tracking Session"**.
4. **Count the cash** in the drawer (you can use the counting tool).
5. **Enter the amount** you counted and click "Next".
6. **Check for discrepancies**: Make a note if any.
7. **Select the float amount** to leave in the register (Retail Shop: $200, Harbor Office: $100).
8. **Remove the stated amount** of cash.
9. **Click "Done"** and print the summary.
10. **Paperclip the summary** to the cash removed and place into the safe. If no cash was removed, just place the summary in the safe.
